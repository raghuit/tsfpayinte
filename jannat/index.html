About Us

Our main motive is to provide relief to those sections of populations which are not easy to be covered. Basic facilities like food, clothing and shelter is provided to these people. Our Secondary goal is to give education to the youngsters. Education of today can make them financially independent. Our members constantly try to reach such people in need . We are doing this since 10 years and wish to continue this as long as 50+ years ! We want every individual who is capable to contribute something as their wish and create a society where we share and live with harmony and peace irrespective of caste,color,creed.


You Can Also Support Us !

In these 10 years of this organisation, we immensely thank all the contributors from the bottom of our heart. The amount of donation does not matter , what matters is your affection towards people and the environment. The fundings we get are always used effectively for the purpose of uplifting lives of the individuals. Proper credit is given to the donor and we also share a memorable note on our social media handles thanking them!. Its very greatful to know that by 2021 we will have helped around 500,000 people and wish to take this more further.
